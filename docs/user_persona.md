# USER PERSONA 

## 1. Role and Context 

- Job title: Financial Compliance Officer 
- Responsible for reviewing flagged advisor trades/recommendations for suitability and regulatory compliance 

## 2. Core Goals 

- Identify and escalate high-risk or non-compliant recommendations  
- Prioritize review workload effectively  
- Make consistent, defensible decisions  
- Complete reviews efficiently  
- Maintain audit-ready documentation 

## 3. Current Workflow 

- Received alert from surveillance system 
- Gather and review relevant data from multiple systems (trade details, client profile, portfolio composition, advisor transaction history, client/advisor communication history, regulatory and compliance policy documentation) 
- Interpret policies and assess compliance and suitability 
- Decide to approve, escalate, or reject transaction 
- Document review and decision rationale 

## 4. Pain Points 

- High volume of alerts leading to backlog pressure 
- Need to manually prioritize cases in real time reduces overall case throughput 
- Fragmented data across systems 
- Ambiguous or context-dependent policies 
- Limited time per case 
- Lack of visibility into similar past decisions 

## 5. Decision Drivers 

- Avoiding missing true compliance violations  
- Ensuring decisions are defensible under audit  
- Relying on clear, evidence-backed reasoning  
- Maintaining consistency with prior decisions 

## 6. Behaviours & Tendencies 

- Risk-averse; prefers escalation over missing risk  
- Uses heuristics under time pressure  
- Prefers explainable systems  
- Initially skeptical of automation 

## 7. Trust & Adoption Factors 

- Trust increases with clear, consistent, correct, and explainable (cited) outputs 
- Trust decreases with confidently incorrect, unnecessarily escalated, or opaque outputs 

## 8. Attributes 

- 3–10 years of compliance experience  
- Familiar with firm policies and regulatory frameworks
