# Risk Register

|ID|Risk|Type|Status|Current signal|Impact|Mitigation / response|Owner|
|--|----|----|------|--------------|------|---------------------|-----|
|Risk_1|Compliant cases unnecessarily escalated to review|Workflow / UX|Open|Auto-pass over-escalation remains a key failure mode|Lower reviewer efficiency and reduced perceived product value|Tune auto-pass criteria while preserving false-negative and urgent-case safeguards; evaluate over-routing by scenario|PM / ML Engineer|
|Risk_2|Non-compliant cases incorrectly treated as compliant|Regulatory / Safety|Reduced, monitor|Compliance false-negative rate is a core safety metric; conservative routing is used to reduce this risk|Missed compliance violations, regulatory exposure, and loss of trust|Maintain conservative safeguards; track false-negative rate and unsafe auto-pass rate; validate material changes on held-out data|PM / ML Engineer|
|Risk_3|Retrieval fails to surface relevant or primary policy evidence|Retrieval / Model|Open|Primary-policy retrieval and selected-policy precision remain important constraints|Weak evidence quality, weaker rationale, and reduced reviewer confidence|Improve retrieval, reranking, chunking, and policy-specific diagnostics; evaluate downstream impact on workflow routing|ML Engineer|
|Risk_4|AI rationale is hallucinated or insufficiently grounded|Model / Trust|Open|Retrieved policy evidence is displayed, but rationale grounding is not yet scored as a dedicated metric|Loss of trust and risk of reviewer reliance on unsupported explanations|Use evidence display and audit logs; future work would add rationale-grounding checks or sampled human review|PM / ML Engineer|
|Risk_5|Confidence proxy is poorly calibrated|Model / Trust|Open|Confidence is a deterministic proxy, not the model's internal probability; calibration evidence is limited by few incorrect cases|Reviewer over-reliance or under-reliance on system recommendations|Track overconfidence, confidence/correctness alignment, and calibration diagnostics; avoid presenting confidence as a fully validated probability|ML Engineer|
|Risk_6|Policy evidence or case facts are misinterpreted by AI-extracted signals|Model / Product|Open|Signal-level ground truth is not yet labelled and evaluated|Incorrect scoring inputs may lead to incorrect routing or misleading rationale|Future work would add ground-truth signal labels and evaluate signal extraction precision/recall|PM / ML Engineer|
|Risk_7|Reviewer trust does not improve with use|Product / UX|Open|Trust proxy is simulated and not validated with real reviewers|Low adoption or inappropriate reliance|Treat trust proxy as analytical only; prioritize explanation clarity, predictable routing, and reviewer control; validate with representative users before operational use|PM|
|Risk_8|Urgent cases are routed below intended urgency|Product / Safety|Open|Urgent-case recall is a key workflow-safety metric|Highest-risk cases may not receive timely review attention|Review urgency thresholds and routing rules; validate changes on held-out data|PM / ML Engineer|
|Risk_9|Retrieval improvements do not improve end-to-end workflow routing|Evaluation / Product|Open|Retrieval benchmark can improve retrieval metrics without materially improving the North Star Metric|Engineering effort may optimize subsystem metrics without improving product outcomes|Use workflow-routing accuracy and failure-mode analysis as final product decision criteria|PM|
|Risk_10|Synthetic ground-truth labels do not match expert compliance judgment|Validation|Open|Labels are generated using deterministic project rules rather than expert adjudication|Offline results may not reflect real-world compliance decisions|Review labels, thresholds, and workflow assumptions with compliance-domain experts before operational use|PM / Domain Expert|
|Risk_11|Reviewer collaboration gaps create duplicate work|Workflow / UX|Identified for future scope|MVP does not include case ownership or in-review locking|Multiple reviewers may review the same case or lose visibility into ownership|Add assignment, ownership, and in-review status controls in a future workflow iteration|PM / Frontend Engineer|
|Risk_12|Schema or structured-output failures interrupt downstream scoring|Engineering / Model|Partially mitigated, monitor|Structured outputs are used by the pipeline, but schema-validity reporting is not yet surfaced as a dedicated evaluation metric|Pipeline errors or incomplete evidence extraction|Maintain schema validation and error handling; future work would report parsing success/failure rates|ML Engineer|
